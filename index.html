My Naked Finances

If you're living paycheck to paycheck...

the chances of you having a pile of money to work with, even in the form of an emergency fund, is slim to none. 77% of workers are living paycheck to paycheck and it seems that financial education and tools are not enough.

The fact is that we dont tend to teach or discuss finances, not to mention sound ways to manage finances, unless a financial problem arises. Even then, pride and being overwhelmed from busy lives often prevent us from seeking the help that we need.

Wealthy people use financial advisors, money coaches and daily money managers, but they too are not free. They may charge annual, monthly, or hourly rates or they may be required to sell you financial or insurance products in order to pay for the services that are marketed to you as free. Even then, it can takes months, sometimes years, to make meaningful progress.

Are you willing to get financially naked so that others may learn?

Share your journey to better financial health and get the help you need to succeed. At My Naked Finances, LLC, our goal is financial health for the masses a little at a time through transparency, creative thinking, and collaboration. To that end, for those who qualify, we present the My Naked Finances program which includes the following.

6 months of money coaching on your behalf, but at no cost to you.*

Challenges that give you the opportunity to create an emergency fund, reduce expenses, and ultimately build your pile of cash.

Published video and audio of your financial journey, including successes, failures, and personal journal entries so that family and friends can support you and others may learn from the journey.

*W-9 required. Youll receive a 1099 the following year for the value of the services provided.
