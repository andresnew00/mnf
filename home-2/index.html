Naked Finances on the Street

Share your story, get the help you need, and earn a chance to win!

"Would you be willing to share your financial struggles with the world if it meant getting help and cash to address those struggles?"

That is the question that we intend to answer. Consider it a never ending poll of the public to find out who thinks that the trade off is worth it and why. We interview people on the street, in other public spaces, and at events. All it takes is 3 simple steps.

STEP 1

Visit My Naked Finances home page. Follow, like, and subscribe to our YouTube channel plus your preferred social media site.

STEP 2

Tag us in a post on your preferred social media platform, with the title: "I'm looking for My Naked Finances on the street. WYA?"

STEP 3

Find us at one of the listed events below or at a pop up event and be interviewed.

And for your trouble...

Youll be entered into a drawing to win a $25 Amazon gift card. If youve signed up for one of the events below at least 24 hours before participating in a Naked Finances on the Street interview and you win, well double the Amazon gift card to $50. Drawings to determine the winner are held on the 27th of each month.*

* Winning contestants are limited to 1 prize per calendar year.

Congratulations to Our Winner, Sunshine (Nesia)!

This month, the lucky gift card winner is Sunshine (Nesia). Press play to hear her interview, and catch a new round of money tips. If you want your name in the next drawing, sign up for our next event! Good luck and keep those questions coming.
